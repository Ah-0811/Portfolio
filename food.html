Egyptian Cuisine

Delicious dishes passed down through generations

Koshari

A hearty mix of pasta, rice, lentils, and tomato sauce topped with crispy onions and vinegar.

Ful Medames

Mashed fava beans flavored with olive oil, garlic, and lemon, often served for breakfast.

Baklava

Sweet and flaky pastry filled with nuts and soaked in syrup, popular throughout Egypt.
