About Alexandria

Alexandria is a vibrant port city founded by Alexander the Great, blending Egyptian, Greek, and Roman heritage with a Mediterranean lifestyle.

Must-See Attractions

Bibliotheca Alexandrina: A modern library honoring the ancient Library of Alexandria.

Qaitbay Citadel: A historic fortress on the Mediterranean coast with stunning views.

Montazah Palace Gardens: Beautiful gardens along the shore, perfect for a relaxing stroll.

Food and Cuisine

Sample fresh seafood, traditional Egyptian dishes, and Mediterranean-influenced cuisine in the local markets and seaside restaurants.

Travel Tips

Walk along the Corniche for scenic views, explore early in the day to avoid crowds, and try public ferries to nearby islands for a unique experience.
