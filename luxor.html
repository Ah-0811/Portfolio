About Luxor

Luxor, often called the world's greatest open-air museum, is home to extraordinary temples, tombs, and monuments of ancient Egypt.

Must-See Attractions

Valley of the Kings: Discover the burial sites of pharaohs, including Tutankhamun.

Karnak Temple: A massive complex of temples, chapels, and pylons dedicated to the gods of Thebes.

Luxor Temple: A majestic temple in the heart of the city, beautifully illuminated at night.

Food and Cuisine

Try local delicacies such as grilled fish from the Nile, koshari, and traditional sweets at Luxor's markets.

Travel Tips

Hire a guide to fully understand the history, carry water and sun protection, and plan early morning or late afternoon visits to avoid the heat.
