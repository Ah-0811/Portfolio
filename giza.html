About Giza

Giza, located just outside Cairo, is world-famous for its ancient monuments, including the Great Pyramids and the Sphinx, representing the height of ancient Egyptian architectural achievement.

Must-See Attractions

The Great Pyramid of Khufu: The largest and oldest of the three pyramids at Giza, a marvel of ancient engineering.

The Sphinx: Iconic limestone statue with the body of a lion and the head of a pharaoh.

Pyramid of Khafre & Pyramid of Menkaure: Explore the other two major pyramids and their associated temples.

Pyramids Sound and Light Show: Evening shows provide a spectacular storytelling experience of Giza's history.

Food and Cuisine

Nearby Cairo offers authentic Egyptian dishes, street food like koshari, and restaurants serving local specialties with views of the pyramids.

Travel Tips

Arrive early to avoid crowds and heat, wear comfortable shoes for walking around the pyramids, and hire a licensed guide to fully enjoy the history and stories of Giza.
