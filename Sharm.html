About Sharm El Sheikh

Sharm El Sheikh is a popular resort city on the Red Sea, famous for its clear waters, coral reefs, and luxurious hotels.

Must-See Attractions

Ras Mohammed National Park: Stunning coral reefs, marine life, and natural landscapes for snorkeling and diving.

Naama Bay: Bustling promenade with restaurants, shops, and vibrant nightlife.

Mount Sinai and St. Catherine Monastery: Day trips from Sharm for historical and spiritual exploration.

Food and Cuisine

Enjoy fresh seafood, international cuisine, and local Egyptian dishes at seaside restaurants and resorts.

Travel Tips

Bring swimwear and sunscreen, plan diving or snorkeling trips in advance, and use local taxis or shuttle services to get around safely.
